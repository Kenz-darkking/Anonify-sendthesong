Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is SendTheSong?

SendTheSong is a platform for sharing anonymous heartfelt messages paired with songs from Spotify. It's a creative way to express untold emotions and connect with others through music and words.

🎵 Can I really send anonymous messages?

Yes! SendTheSong is completely anonymous. We don't store any sender information. The only information recorded is the recipient's name, the message, and the song details.

🔍 How do I find messages sent to me?

Go to the "Browse" page and search for your name (or any name you'd like). All messages with that recipient name will appear.

🎶 Where do the songs come from?

All songs are sourced from Spotify's massive music library. You can search for any song and add it to your message.

💾 Are my messages saved forever?

Yes, all messages are saved and can be found by searching the recipient's name. They're part of the platform's history.

🚫 Can I delete my message?

Currently, messages cannot be deleted once published. Please think carefully before sending!

📱 Is SendTheSong mobile-friendly?

Yes! The entire platform is designed to work beautifully on mobile devices.

🛡️ Is my privacy protected?

Absolutely. This is an anonymous platform. We do not store personal identity of senders.
